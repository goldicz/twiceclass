Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you contact us

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. However, our WhatsApp support is available 24/7.

We respond to WhatsApp messages within minutes during business hours. Email inquiries are typically answered within 24 hours. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours.

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure our financial advisors are available to give you personalized attention. You can book an appointment via WhatsApp or phone.

Please bring a valid ID, bank statements (3 months), proof of income, utility bill, and your BVN. Having these documents ready will help us process your application faster.
